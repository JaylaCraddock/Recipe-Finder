Popular Dishes

Cassava Pizza

Cassava Pizza is a Italian gluten-free version of a regular pizza that features the cassava vegetable that is used to create the crust. While maintaining the crispy and chewy texture of a traditional pizza.

Clam Chowder

Soup that is made with clams and potatoes with the base being made with either milk or tomatoes. In addition to having ingredients such as salt pork, bacon and onions.

Chicken Handi

Indian dish that features a rich and creamy spicy chicken curry that is made out of ingredients such as: marinated chicken, tomatoes, yogurt, cream, garlic, turmeric, ginger, coriander leaves and chilli powder.
